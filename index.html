What type of catering do you offer?

We have pre-set platters, where bagels are sliced vertically which is excellent for sharing at parties or events where everyone is seated around the same table.

We also offer normally-sliced bagels with tubs of cream cheese, or pre-made sandwiches. Please let us know if you are looking for something else, and we can likely accommodate it!

Is there a minimum order?

Nope! We are happy to take orders of any size.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Please let us know ahead of time about dietary restrictions. We will coordinate and confirm whether we can accommodate dietary restrictions.

Do you deliver?

Yes, delivery in Denver is included with all catering orders.

Do you require a deposit?

For the majority of orders, no deposit is required. Payment is made at the time of delivery by credit card or check.
