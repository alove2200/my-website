What type of catering do you offer?

We offer many types of catering! We can create platters with bagels sliced vertically "St. Louis style," which is great for sharing and tasting different types of bagels and cream cheeses. We can also create individual bags with cream cheese. Just let us know what you're looking for and we'll be happy to accommodate.

Is there a minimum order?

Nope! We are happy to take orders of any size.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Please let us know ahead of time about dietary restrictions. We will coordinate and confirm whether we can accommodate your needs.

Do you deliver?

Yes, delivery in Denver is included with all catering orders.

Do you require a deposit?

For the majority of orders, no deposit is required. Payment can be made ahead of time or at time of delivery by card or check.
